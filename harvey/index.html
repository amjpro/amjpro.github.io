About Me

Welcome to my homepage! I have extensive experience in C/C++ software development, from additive manufacturing software at Oak Ridge National Laboratory's Manufacturing Demonstration Facility to programs for real-time digital signal processing at Idaho National Laboratory. Additionally, I have experience with Python and Bash scripting, web development, and firmware engineering for embedded systems.

I earned a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and a Master's in Computer Science from North Carolina State University. I've studied and written programs for cybersecurity, bioinformatics, graphics, IoT, and machine learning.

At Chapel Hill, I also minored in both neuroscience and music. As a musician trained in piano, classical guitar, and voice in a variety of genres from classical to jazz, I've performed at various events, transcribed existing songs to sheet music, and composed original pieces.
